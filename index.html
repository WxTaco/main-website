What Is Wrapped?

Wrapped is our version of a multi-purpose discord bot designed with you in mind! We are dedicated to developing a bot that has the best user experience possible, and we accompish that by putting you, the user, first!

All we ask is that you bear with us through the development process, as our development team is small at the momment. We appreciate your patience and committment to Wrapped and are excited to share the project with you!
