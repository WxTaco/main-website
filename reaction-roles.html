reaction roles feature!

A simple and easy to use system for reaction roles, perfect for a quick setup!

How Does It Work?

Reaction roles with Wrapped are simple, just run /reaction roles , fill out the "message-link" and "reaction" options, and then you're all set!

Note: some default emojis may not be supported at this momment, a fix is being developed for this.
